Design Engineering-

A design engineer usually works with a team of engineers and other designers to develop conceptual and detailed designs that ensure a product actually functions, performs and is fit for its purpose. Design engineers are the bridge between making things that are extremely powerful and useful while still being aesthetic, and attractive to users. They also understand and communicate the architecture of complex problems; they study and engage a variety of situations with the technical and critical skills needed to design new products that solve hard problems.
