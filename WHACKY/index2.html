Joy Buolamwini

Computer Scientist & Activist

Bio-

Joy Buolamwini is a computer scientist. She has studied at Georgia Tech, Oxford University, and is currently pursuing her PH.D at the MIT media lab. Joy is a Rhodes Scholar, a Fulbright Fellow, an Astronaut Scholar, a Google Anita Borg Scholar, and a Carter Center technical consultant recognized as a distinguished volunteer. She is also extremely passionate about social justice which has lead her to start projects such as the Algorithmic Justice League to fight bias in machine learning and Code4Rights which supports youth in creating meaningful technology for their communities in partnership with local organization.
