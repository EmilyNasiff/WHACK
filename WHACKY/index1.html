Mayim Bialik

Neuroscientist & Actress

Bio-

Mayim Bialik is a neuroscientist and an actress. Mayim received her PH.D in neuroscience from UCLA in 2007. Her dissertation was an investigation of hypothalamic activity in patients with Prader–Willi syndrome. Mayim is currently cast as Dr. Amy Farrah Fowler (a fellow neuroscientist) on CBS’s The Big Bang Theory.

Neuroscience-

Is the study of the brain (the most complex, known living structure). Neuroscientists often study neurological disorders which affect up to one billion people worldwide.
